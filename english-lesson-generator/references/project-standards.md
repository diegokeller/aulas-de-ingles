# Project Standards for English Lessons (Intermediate pt-BR)

## Audience
- **Target:** Intermediate (B1-B2)
- **Native Language:** Brazilian Portuguese (pt-BR)
- **Goal:** Practical conversational English and vocabulary.

## Language Balance
- **Portuguese:** Instructions, grammar explanations, goals, and disambiguation notes.
- **English:** Vocabulary, example sentences, exercises, and reading texts.
- **Key Terms:** **Bold**.
- **Example Sentences:** *Italics*.

## Technical Standards (Print Optimized)
- **Format:** HTML5 with Bootstrap 5 CDN.
- **Font Size:** 12pt (standard for A4).
- **Writing Spaces:** Use `<hr class="writing-line">` for multi-line and `<span class="inline-blank"></span>` for inline blanks.
- **No Dots:** Do not use `....` for blanks; use CSS-defined classes.

## Grammar Topics
New lessons should pick from future suggestions:
- Present Continuous for Future (Travel/Plans)
- Comparative & Superlative (Comparing cities/hotels/food)
- Past Simple (Regular/Irregular)
- Present Perfect (Experience - "Have you ever...?")
- First Conditional (Environment/Plans/Possibilities)
- Gerund vs. Infinitive (Hobbies/Preferences)
- Used to (Past habits vs. Present)
