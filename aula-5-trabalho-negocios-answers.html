Exercise 1: Vocabulary Selection

ItemDescription
ColleagueA person you work with.
DeadlineThe latest time or date by which something should be completed.
FeedbackInformation or comments about a person's performance.
BudgetThe amount of money available for a specific purpose.
CEOThe highest-ranking person in a company.
SalaryA fixed regular payment made by an employer to an employee.
MeetingAn assembly of people for a formal discussion.
PromotionAn advancement in rank or position within a company.

Exercise 3: Personal Response

*Answers will vary based on student's experience.*

  • 1. What is your current job and what are your main tasks?
    (Expect: I am a [Job] and I [Tasks].)
  • 2. What are your goals for next week?
    (Expect: My goals are... / I want to finish...)
  • 3. How much time do you spend in meetings every day?
    (Expect: I spend [Time] in meetings.)

Exercise 4: Dictation Answers

1. Hi, I need to reschedule our 2 PM meeting. I have a problem with the budget.

2. No problem. Does that change the deadline for the final report?

3. Yes, it's now next Friday. I need some more time to get the final feedback from the Manager.

4. Understood. Let's send the new agenda ASAP.

Exercise 6: Conversation

Exercise 7: Reading Comprehension Answers

  1. 1. Who called the meeting? The manager.
  2. 2. What needs to be delivered? A complete report.
  3. 3. Why improve workflow? Because the deadline is very tight.
  4. 4. What was discussed/agreed? The budget and that it is a win-win situation.
  5. 5. When for the first draft? ASAP (As soon as possible).
