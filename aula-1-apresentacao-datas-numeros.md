# Lesson 1: Introductions, Dates, and Numbers

---

## Goals

- Praticar **introductions** (apresentações pessoais) e vocabulário essencial.
- Aprender os **months of the year** (meses do ano) e **days of the week** (dias da semana).
- Entender como dizer **dates** (datas) usando **ordinal numbers** (números ordinais).
- Desenvolver habilidades de **conversation** (conversação).

---

## 1. Warm-up & Personal Introduction

Vamos praticar a conversação e nos conhecer melhor!

### Reading

Leia o texto abaixo, que é uma autoapresentação de um aluno fictício:

> Hello everyone! My name is John and I am really excited to start this intermediate English course with all of you. I'm 28 years old, and I live in Spain. I work as a freelancer. I started learning English because I love traveling and I want to communicate fluently when I visit other countries.
>
> My birthday is on July 17th. My favorite month is July because I always take a vacation in the summer. I usually go to the beach or visit some friends and family. This year, my goal is to improve my speaking skills and be able to hold a conversation without thinking too much about the grammar rules.
>
> I'm looking forward to learning from you all! What about you?

---

### How to Say Dates

Existem duas formas principais de dizer uma data:

| Style | Format | Example | How to Say |
|-------|--------|---------|------------|
| **American** (US) | Month + Day | May 10th | "May tenth" or "May the tenth" |
| **British** (UK) | Day + Month | 10th May | "The tenth of May" |

*Examples:*
- *July 4th = July fourth (US) / The fourth of July (UK)*
- *December 25th = December twenty-fifth (US) / The twenty-fifth of December (UK)*

## 5. Conversation Practice (Role Play)

Prepare-se para conversar com um colega ou professor.

### Topics for Dialogue:

1. **Introduce yourself** (name, age, where you're from, what you do)
2. **Ask and tell** your favorite month/day of the week and why
3. **Ask and answer:** "When is your birthday?"

---
