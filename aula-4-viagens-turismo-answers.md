### Exercise 4: Dictation Exercise (Exercício de Ditado)

**Part A: Fill in the blanks**
1. Could you tell me where the **check-in counter** is?
2. I’d like to **book a room** for two nights, please.
3. Does this **bus** go to the **city center**?

**Part B: Comprehension**
**Audio Transcript:** *"Excuse me, could you tell me where the nearest pharmacy is? I need to buy some medicine for my headache. Also, is there a bank nearby? I need to withdraw some cash."*

1. What is the first place the speaker is looking for?
   **Answer:** The speaker is looking for the **pharmacy**.

2. What is the speaker's health problem?
   **Answer:** The speaker has a **headache**.

3. Why does the speaker need to find a bank?
   **Answer:** The speaker needs to **withdraw some cash**.

### Exercise 5: Rewriting for Politeness

1. Give me a map of the city. (**Could**)
   *Answer: Could you give me a map of the city?*

2. I want a glass of water. (**Would**)
   *Answer: I would like a glass of water, please. / Would you bring me a glass of water?*

3. Open the window for me. (**Can**)
   *Answer: Can you open the window for me?*

4. Tell me the password for the Wi-Fi. (**Could**)
   *Answer: Could you tell me the password for the Wi-Fi?*

5. Help me with this heavy suitcase. (**Would**)
   *Answer: Would you mind helping me with this heavy suitcase?*

### Exercise 8: Conversation Practice - Giving Directions

*Note: Answers for directions may vary depending on the chosen path. All directions start from the **"You are here"** sign.*

1. **Hospital:**
   - **Question:** *Excuse me, could you tell me how to get to the Hospital?*
   - **Directions:** Go straight on Bond Street. At the traffic lights, turn left into King's Road. Go past the Cinema and the Post Office. The Hospital is at the end of the street, on the left.

2. **Bus Station:**
   - **Question:** *Hello, could you help me? I'm looking for the Bus Station.*
   - **Directions:** Go straight on Bond Street. Go past the Cafe. The Bus Station is on your left, next to the Underground Station.

3. **Factory:**
   - **Question:** *Excuse me, would you mind telling me how to get to the Factory?*
   - **Directions:** Go straight on Bond Street. At the traffic lights, turn right into King's Road. Go past the Bookshop. The Factory is at the end of the street, on the right.

### Exercise 9: Reading Comprehension Questions

1. Where is Lucas from and which city is he visiting?
   *Answer: Lucas is from Brazil and he is visiting New York City.*

2. What was Lucas's goal for his first day of exploration?
   *Answer: His goal was to visit Central Park and the Metropolitan Museum of Art.*

3. Why did Lucas need to find a machine at the station?
   *Answer: He needed to find a machine to buy (book) a new MetroCard (subway pass).*

4. How did Lucas find his way to the museum?
   *Answer: He asked a local woman for directions using his English skills.*

5. What polite request did Lucas use when talking to the local woman?
   *Answer: Lucas said: "Excuse me, could you help me? I'm looking for the museum."*

6. Where is the museum located according to the woman's directions?
   *Answer: It is on 5th Avenue, two blocks away, turning right, opposite the park entrance.*

7. What did Lucas do inside the museum?
   *Answer: He bought souvenirs for his family and took many photos of the paintings.*

8. What did Lucas learn from his experience of being lost?
   *Answer: He learned that traveling is easier when you aren't afraid to ask for help.*
