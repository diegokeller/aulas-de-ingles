Lesson 7: Health and Medical Appointments

Goals

  • Aprender o vocabulary essencial para descrever sintomas e interagir em um ambiente médico.
  • Praticar o uso de modal verbs para dar conselhos de saúde e expressar obrigatoriedade.
  • Diferenciar termos confusos e false friends comuns na área da saúde.

Disambiguation

Prescription vs. RecipePrescription = médica.
Recipe = culinária.
Illness vs. DiseaseIllness = sensação de estar doente.
Disease = condição específica.

False Friends

PhysicianNão é físico, é médico.
InjuryNão é injúria, é ferimento/lesão.
AssistNão é assistir, é ajudar.

Grammar: Modal Verbs (Advice & Necessity)

Usamos modais para diferentes níveis de conselho, regra ou proibição.

UsagePositiveNegative
Advice (Conselho)You should rest.You shouldn't smoke.
Obligation (Regra/Lei)He has to wait.I don't have to pay.
Strong/ProhibitionYou must stop.You mustn't drive.

Reading Comprehension

Last Monday, John woke up feeling very under the weather. He had the Monday morning blues, but it was more than just being tired; he had a terrible headache and a sore throat. His neck was a bit swollen, and he felt dizzy when he tried to stand up. He also noticed a large bruise on his arm, but he couldn't remember how he got it.

He called his physician and made an appointment. At the clinic, the GP told him: "You must rest. You should drink water and you shouldn't go to work. I will give you a prescription for a strong painkiller." The pharmacist warned that he mustn't drive after taking it due to side effects.
